Humanoid Loco-Manipulation System

Shanghai AI Lab Internship · 09/2025–Present

Developed a unified reinforcement learning policy and teleoperation system for humanoid robot loco-manipulation and manipulation without demonstration data, supporting torque-based hybrid force-position control across the whole body.

  • Implemented a high-frequency multimodal data collection pipeline in C++ at 30 Hz.
  • Integrated RGB-D images, LiDAR point clouds, proprioceptive states, SLAM localization, and high-level control commands.
  • Constructed a high-level command generation framework on collected data using diffusion policies and VLA models.
  • Submitted a first-author paper to RSS 2026.

Motion Tracing Methods for Dexterous-Hand Manipulation

Master's Research Project · 09/2024–Present

Research on retargeting and policy learning for dexterous manipulation from MoCap data to articulated object interaction.

  • Optimized the retargeting pipeline from MoCap data to dexterous-hand interactions.
  • Designed reward functions for reinforcement learning on articulated object manipulation.
  • Addressed task expansion challenges using policy distillation modules.
  • Adapted motion generation methods to various kinds of dexterous hands.

Humanoid Robots Playing Soccer

Unitree Internship · 05/2025–08/2025

Contributed technical support to the Tsinghua University THMOS RoboCup team as a Unitree intern and team member, with a focus on motion deployment and decision-making for the Unitree G1 humanoid.

  • Developed decision-making algorithms and trained/deployed fall-recovery motions on the C1 robot.
  • Implemented a 2-DoF head servo camera control pipeline integrated with perception-driven visual tracking.
  • Helped the team win 4th prize at RoboCup 2025 in Brazil.

A 4D World-Model for Robotic Arms Based on Gaussian Splatting

ICRA 2026

Proposed a V-4D-A framework with Gaussian Action Field for direct action inference, showing bi-manual dexterous manipulation capability in RLBench tasks and real-world deployment.

  • Achieved a +11.54 dB PSNR and -0.56 LPIPS improvement.
  • Boosted robotic task success rate by 10.33% over SOTA methods.
  • Accepted by ICRA 2026.
